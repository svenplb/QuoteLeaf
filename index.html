About

QuoteLeaf is a simple, minimalistic typing test, featuring word settings & themes.
This website uses the top 100 most used words in the English language to generate its tests.
You can press tab to restart the typing test while typing or at the result of the test. After completing a test you will be able to see your wpm.
Wpm stands for words per minute.
